My Approach

I use a collaborative, exploratory method to uncover your inner world, incorporating conversation, experiential exercises like chair work, and relational dynamics. My style is warm, open-minded, energetic, holistic, creative, and out-of-the-box.

  • Exploratory: Uncovering patterns with compassion and courage.
  • Emotional Focus: Naming, processing, and transforming feelings that may have been avoided or denied.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Tailored to religious environments and values, emphasizing self-compassion and symbolic reflection.

"The answers are within you; all you need to do is truly listen." Therapy resonates with your lived experience, empowering growth and honoring your voice.
