Return Home to Who You Were Born to Be

Returning Home is an intensive 3-day Shabbaton-style retreat created exclusively for Jewish men who are done playing small and are ready to face their shadows, release old pain, and reconnect with their fire.

Inspired by the transformative depth of Call of the Shofar and Ahdama — yet completely unique — this weekend combines ancient Jewish wisdom with cutting-edge emotional tools.

What You'll Experience

Deep Breathwork

Guided conscious breathing to release stored trauma and access higher states of awareness.

Shadow Work Processes

Safe, powerful group and one-on-one exercises to meet, own, and integrate the parts you've disowned.

Experiential Activities

Blindfold work, carpet work, movement, voice release, and sacred circle sharing — all in a contained, supportive container.

Torah & Brotherhood

Shabbat together, niggunim, real talk, zero judgment — reconnecting to yourself, to Hashem, and to other men.
