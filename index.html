Return Home to Who You Were Born to Be

What You'll Experience

Deep Breathwork

Guided conscious breathing to release stored trauma and access higher states of awareness.

Shadow Work Processes

Safe, powerful group and one-on-one exercises to meet, own, and integrate the parts you've disowned.

Experiential Activities

Blindfold work, carpet work, movement, voice release, and sacred circle sharing in a supportive container.

Torah & Brotherhood

Shabbat together, niggunim, real talk, zero judgment — reconnecting to yourself, to Hashem, and to other men.

Cold Plunge Immersion

Step into the ice. Slow your breath. Meet the part of you that rises under pressure. Our guided cold plunge experience trains resilience, deep nervous system regulation, and the ability to stay calm and powerful in the face of discomfort.
