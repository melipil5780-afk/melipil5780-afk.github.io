Returning Home

“Home” is not a place. It is a state of being — where your body is calm, your heart is open, and your voice speaks truth without fear. This Shabbaton is built for Jewish men who want to reconnect to themselves, release old emotional weight, and rediscover the grounded, strong, centered version of who they truly are.

The Work You Will Do

Over three days, you will move through powerful guided processes — breath, movement, emotional release, men’s council, cold immersion, and connection. This is deep, honest, Jewish soul-work without religious pressure — a space to let go, reset, and return home.

Somatic Breath Journeys

Breathwork that opens the body, releases old tension, and helps you access parts of yourself blocked by years of pressure and holding.

Emotional Breakthrough Work

Guided processes to move grief, anger, fear, shame, desire, and unmet needs — safely, powerfully, and supported by other men.

Authentic Group Process

Deep circles where men speak truth, listen fully, and connect without judgment or performance. Many describe this as “finally being seen.”

Blindfold Movement

A powerful method for dropping from your mind into your body. Movement becomes a doorway into freedom, expression, and clarity.

Cold Immersion Training

Learn to stay present and steady in intensity. Build resilience and deepen your nervous system’s capacity for calm.

Connection & Brotherhood

Men supporting men. Showing up for each other. Holding each other through breakthroughs. This becomes a brotherhood that doesn’t fade.
